#File System Structure
The file system starts at /

Most Distros contain the following folder structure:

+**bin**
+**boot** 
+**dev**
+**etc** 
+**home** 
+**media** 
+**lib** 
+**mnt** 
+**root**
+**usr** 
+**var**
